## Elevator Pitch
Earth Guardian AI is a 14-module environmental intelligence platform that
unifies climate risk, disaster prediction, air and water quality, renewable
energy, biodiversity, and carbon accounting into one command-center interface.
Built with React, TypeScript, and Tailwind, it's designed to be both a
genuinely usable product foundation and a portfolio-ready technical
showcase — complete with an interactive carbon calculator, a custom
telemetry-style design system, and full product/business documentation.
