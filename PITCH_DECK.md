### 1. Title
**Earth Guardian AI** — An operating system for planet Earth.
*Tagline: See environmental risk before it lands.*

### 2. The Problem
Environmental data is fragmented across dozens of agencies and formats.
Decision-makers — mayors, NGOs, founders — don't have five minutes to
synthesize climate, air, water, and disaster data before they need to act.

### 3. The Insight
Every one of these domains already has a "risk score" pattern buried inside
it — but none of them are presented in one legible interface. The opportunity
isn't new science; it's synthesis and UX.

### 4. The Product
